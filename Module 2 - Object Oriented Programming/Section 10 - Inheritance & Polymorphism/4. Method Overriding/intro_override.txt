Override
* A defined method in the Superclass can be overridden by its Subclasses.
* Override means that the Subclass replaces the Superclass' existing implementation.
* The Subclass provides its own implementation for an inherited method from the Superclass.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

@Override annotation
* Ensures that you are actually overriding a method belonging to the Superclass.
